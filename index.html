What Is Boxing?

Boxing is a combat sport where two athletes use footwork, defense, timing and punches to compete under a set of rules. It is not only about power, but also strategy, rhythm, endurance and mental control.

Modern boxing includes different weight divisions, timed rounds, referees, judges and international organizations. WITMASS explains these parts in a simple way for beginners and fans.
