History of Boxing

3000 BC - Ancient Civilizations

Evidence of fist fighting has been found in ancient Egypt and Mesopotamia, making boxing-like combat one of humanity's oldest sports.

688 BC - Ancient Greece

Boxing became an official event in the Ancient Olympic Games. Fighters competed without weight classes and wore leather straps around their hands.

1700s - Boxing in England

Bare-knuckle boxing became popular in England, where prize fights attracted thousands of spectators and the sport began to develop formal rules.

1867 - Queensberry Rules

The Marquess of Queensberry Rules introduced gloves, three-minute rounds and modern regulations, laying the foundation for today's boxing.

1904 - Olympic Boxing

Boxing officially joined the modern Olympic Games and quickly spread across Europe, Asia, Africa and the Americas through international competition.

1930s–1950s - Golden Era

Champions such as Joe Louis brought boxing to global audiences through radio broadcasts and large international events.

1960s - Muhammad Ali Changes Boxing

Muhammad Ali combined incredible athletic skill with charisma and social activism, becoming one of the most famous athletes in history.

1986 - Mike Tyson

Mike Tyson became the youngest heavyweight champion ever at just 20 years old,revolutionizing the heavyweight division with explosive power.

1990s–2000s - Boxing Goes Global

Champions from Mexico, the Philippines, Ukraine, Japan and many other countries proved boxing had become a truly worldwide sport.

Türkiye - Boxing Success

Boxing has grown steadily in Türkiye through amateur competitions and international tournaments. Turkish athletes have earned medals in European Championships, World Championships and the Olympic Games.

Busenaz Sürmeneli

In 2021, Busenaz Sürmeneli became the first Turkish boxer to win Olympic gold, inspiring a new generation of athletes across the country.

Today - Worldwide Boxing

Boxing is now practiced in more than 200 countries, with millions of fans watching professional title fights, Olympic boxing and amateur competitions every year.
